The Story Behind Classical Stories

My journey into wedding photography began in 2015 in the beautiful coastal town of Kasaragod, Kerala. What started as a passion for capturing candid moments quickly evolved into a dedicated profession.

With a background in fine arts and a deep appreciation for Indian wedding traditions, I blend contemporary techniques with timeless composition to create wedding memories that stand the test of time.

Every wedding I photograph is approached with fresh eyes. I believe that each couple has a unique story, and my mission is to document these stories authentically - preserving not just how the day looked, but how it felt.

Over the years, I've had the privilege of documenting hundreds of weddings across India - from intimate ceremonies in Kerala to grand celebrations in Delhi, from beachside weddings in Goa to palatial affairs in Rajasthan.

My Philosophy

Authenticity

I believe in capturing genuine emotions rather than staged moments. My unobtrusive approach allows me to document the real essence of your wedding day.

Artistry

Each photograph is treated as an artwork, with careful attention to composition, lighting, and emotion to create images that are both beautiful and meaningful.

Attention to Detail

The smallest details often tell the biggest stories. I pay close attention to the intricate details that make your wedding unique.

Personal Connection

Building a relationship with my couples allows me to better understand their vision and create photographs that truly reflect their personalities.

My Approach

I blend three distinct styles in my wedding photography:

  • Documentary: Capturing the authentic, unscripted moments throughout your day.
  • Editorial: Creating stylized, magazine-worthy portraits that highlight your connection.
  • Traditional: Ensuring those important family photos and key moments are beautifully documented.

On your wedding day, I work discreetly, allowing events to unfold naturally while ensuring I'm always in position to capture the important moments. My goal is to be invisible enough that you forget about the camera, yet present enough that no special moment goes undocumented.

After your wedding, I personally oversee the editing process, carefully selecting and enhancing each image to create a cohesive story of your day. I pride myself on delivering images that remain timeless, avoiding trendy filters that may look dated in years to come.
