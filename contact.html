Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?

+

I recommend booking 6-12 months in advance for wedding dates, especially during peak season (October-February). For pre-wedding shoots, 2-3 months notice is usually sufficient.

Do you travel outside Kerala?

+

Absolutely! I cover weddings across India and am available for destination weddings worldwide. Travel and accommodation charges apply for locations outside Kerala.

What's your photography style?

+

My style blends candid documentary photography with elegant portraiture. I focus on capturing authentic emotions and meaningful moments while ensuring classic, timeless images.

How long until we receive our photos?

+

Digital images are typically delivered within 4-6 weeks after your wedding. For albums and prints, the process takes an additional 4-8 weeks depending on your selections and revisions.

Do you provide both photo and video services?

+

Yes, I offer comprehensive packages that include both photography and videography services. My team includes skilled videographers who work seamlessly with our photography style.
