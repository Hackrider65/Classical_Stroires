Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?

+

We recommend booking 6-12 months in advance for wedding dates, especially for peak wedding season (October-February). Popular dates can book up to 18 months in advance.

Do you travel for weddings?

+

Yes, we travel throughout India and internationally for weddings. Travel fees may apply depending on the location, but we offer special packages for destination weddings.

How many photos will we receive?

+

The number of photos depends on your package and the events covered. Typically, you can expect 50-75 edited images per hour of coverage. All images are carefully selected and professionally edited.

How long until we receive our photos?

+

You'll receive a sneak peek within a week of your wedding. The complete gallery is typically delivered within 6-8 weeks during peak season and 4-6 weeks during off-peak times. Custom albums take an additional 4-6 weeks for design and production.

Do you provide both digital files and printed products?

+

Yes, all our packages include high-resolution digital files with printing rights. We also offer a range of premium printed products including fine art albums, parent albums, prints, and wall art.
